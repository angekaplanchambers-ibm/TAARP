# Exercise 1: User Research

Work with your own raw interview notes or research data.

---

## 1. Planning Research

Prompts to try:

- "spark research plan for [feature or decision]"
- "create a discovery research plan from this product spec"
- "plan a design evaluation study for this prototype"
- "what method and participant count fit these learning goals?"
- "fill out this SPARK research plan prompt from my inputs"

---

## 2. Generating Questions

Prompts to try:

- "generate follow-up questions based on these raw notes"
- "what questions should I ask in the next interview?"
- "what gaps do I have in my understanding of this user?"

---

## 3. Converting Raw Notes into Answers

Prompts to try:

- "read the raw notes and fill in the interview template"
- "extract answers from the transcript and place them in the right sections"
- "convert this voice-to-text dump into structured notes"

---

## 4. Creating Insights

Prompts to try:

- "synthesize these notes into key insights"
- "create a one-page UXR synthesis from this interview"
- "write a JTBD statement based on what the participant said"
- "create an empathy map from this interview"
- "what mental models does this user have?"

---

## 5. Updating Principles

Prompts to try:

- "what design principles emerge from this research?"
- "create a design principles doc based on these insights"
- "add wireframe mockups to illustrate each principle"
- "what anti-patterns should we avoid based on this research?"

---

## Output

Save your outputs as you go:
- `research-plan.md`
- `uxr-synthesis.md`
- `design-principles.md`
